About Us

Our story, our passion, our vision

Our Story

Founded in 1990, Elegant Exposure has grown from a small photography studio in the heart of Kolkata to one of the most respected names in the industry. For over three decades, we have been capturing life's most precious moments with artistic precision and emotional depth.

What started as a passion project by our founder has evolved into a team of skilled photographers and visual artists who share a common vision: to create timeless imagery that tells stories and preserves memories for generations to come.

Throughout our journey, we have had the privilege of documenting thousands of weddings, events, and milestones for families and businesses across India. Each project has contributed to our growth and refined our approach to visual storytelling.

Meet Our Founder

Rajiv Sharma

Founder & Lead Photographer

With a career spanning over 35 years, Rajiv Sharma has established himself as one of Kolkata's most distinguished photographers. His journey began with a simple film camera gifted by his father, which sparked a lifelong passion for capturing moments and telling stories through images.

After formal training at the prestigious National Institute of Photography, Rajiv worked with several renowned photographers before establishing Elegant Exposure in 1990. His distinctive style combines technical excellence with an artistic eye for composition and light.

Rajiv's work has been featured in numerous publications, and he has received multiple awards for his contributions to photography. Today, he leads a team of talented photographers, sharing his knowledge and vision while continuing to innovate and push creative boundaries.

"Photography is not just about capturing what exists, but revealing what lies beneath – the emotions, the connections, the fleeting moments that tell our human story."
